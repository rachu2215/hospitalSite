Our Services

Cardiology

Our hospital's cardio service provides a range of diagnostic and treatment options for heart conditions, including ECG, echocardiography, stress testing, and cardiac catheterization. Our experienced cardiologists work closely with our patients to develop individualized care plans and provide ongoing support for heart health.



Dermatology

Our hospital's dermatology service offers comprehensive skin care, including diagnosis and treatment of skin conditions such as acne, eczema, and psoriasis. Our skilled dermatologists provide services such as skin cancer screening, mole removal, and cosmetic procedures. We also offer personalized skin care recommendations and education to help our patients maintain healthy skin.



Neurology

Our hospital's neuro service offers advanced diagnostic and treatment options for neurological disorders, such as stroke, epilepsy, and multiple sclerosis. Our team of neurologists and neurosurgeons use state-of-the-art technology to provide personalized care, including brain and spine surgery, electroencephalography, and neuro-rehabilitation. We are committed to improving the quality of life for our patients with neurological conditions.



Orthopedics

Our hospital's ortho service provides comprehensive care for musculoskeletal conditions, such as joint pain, fractures, and sports injuries. Our skilled orthopedic surgeons use advanced techniques and technology to offer personalized treatment options, including joint replacement surgery, arthroscopy, and physical therapy. We are dedicated to helping our patients regain mobility and improve their quality of life.
